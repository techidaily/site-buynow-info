##  Factors to Consider Before Buying a Google Pixel 9 Pro XL Case

 The primary purpose of using a case with any phone is protection. To get the best protection, you should first consider the materials the case consists of. [Thermoplastic polyurethane (TPU)](https://win-blog.techidaily.com/troubleshoot-steamvr-issues-in-minutes-with-this-simple-guide/) is a common material in quality protective cases. It‚Äôs flexible, durable, impact‚Äîand scratch-resistant, and gives a more ‚Äògrippy‚Äô texture. Other durable materials include polycarbonate (PC), aramid fiber, and leather, the latter offering good defense while looking stylish.

 You‚Äôll also want to consider raised lips around the display and camera lens to ensure the glass doesn‚Äôt touch any surface when placed or dropped face down. You‚Äôll also benefit from military-grade drop protection and reinforced corners, although cases like these tend to be on the bulkier, less attractive side.

 However, while thin cases usually offer a lower level of protection, they can still do a good job. They also provide better aesthetics and are less bulky in your hand or pocket. Clear cases are also a go if you prefer the look of your Pixel phone over that of the case. Pixel phones have a unique look, and you might want to avoid covering it up.

 Then, you should consider how you want your stand to function. Do you want it to double as a wallet? Plenty of wallet and folio cases will do a job protecting your phone. Do you [watch movies on your phone](https://extra-support.techidaily.com/2024-approved-masterful-transitioning-8-leading-convertors-for-sub-and-srt/)? In that case, you might want to check that it has a kickstand for hands-free viewing.

 You may even want to charge your phone without the inconvenience of removing it from the case. In this instance, you should buy a phone that is compatible with your preferred method of charging. Additionally, ensure the case has a built-in magnet if you want to attach it to a magnetic [car mount](https://howto.techidaily.com/7-fixes-for-unfortunately-phone-has-stopped-on-itel-p55-drfone-by-drfone-fix-android-problems-fix-android-problems/).

 Lastly, make sure your desired phone case is cut for the Google Pixel 9 Pro XL. The XL is the largest in the [Google Pixel 9 Series](https://hardware-updates.techidaily.com/1722964871657-install-logitechs-high-performance-g29-driving-force-racing-wheel-on-your-pc-driver-support-for-win7-8-and-10/), so read reviews and make sure the cut is precise and you have full access to all buttons and ports.
