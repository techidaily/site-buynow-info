Factors to Consider Before Buying a Google Pixel 9 Pro XL Case

The primary purpose of using a case with any phone is protection. To get the best protection, you should first consider the materials the case consists of. Thermoplastic polyurethane (TPU) is a common material in quality protective cases. It’s flexible, durable, impact—and scratch-resistant, and gives a more ‘grippy’ texture. Other durable materials include polycarbonate (PC), aramid fiber, and leather, the latter offering good defense while looking stylish.

You’ll also want to consider raised lips around the display and camera lens to ensure the glass doesn’t touch any surface when placed or dropped face down. You’ll also benefit from military-grade drop protection and reinforced corners, although cases like these tend to be on the bulkier, less attractive side.

However, while thin cases usually offer a lower level of protection, they can still do a good job. They also provide better aesthetics and are less bulky in your hand or pocket. Clear cases are also a go if you prefer the look of your Pixel phone over that of the case. Pixel phones have a unique look, and you might want to avoid covering it up.

Then, you should consider how you want your stand to function. Do you want it to double as a wallet? Plenty of wallet and folio cases will do a job protecting your phone. Do you watch movies on your phone ? In that case, you might want to check that it has a kickstand for hands-free viewing.

You may even want to charge your phone without the inconvenience of removing it from the case. In this instance, you should buy a phone that is compatible with your preferred method of charging. Additionally, ensure the case has a built-in magnet if you want to attach it to a magnetic car mount .

Lastly, make sure your desired phone case is cut for the Google Pixel 9 Pro XL. The XL is the largest in the Google Pixel 9 Series , so read reviews and make sure the cut is precise and you have full access to all buttons and ports.

FAQ

What is the difference between the Google Pixel 9 Pro and Google Pixel 9 Pro XL?

The main difference is the size. The Google Pixel 9 Pro XL is the largest in the series. It has dimensions of 6.4 x 3 x 0.33 inches (162.8 x 76.6 x 8.5mm) and a weight of 7.8oz (221g) compared to the Pixel 9 Pro’s 6 x 2.8 x 0.33 inches (152.8 x 72.0 x 8.5mm) and 7oz (199g) weight.

Furthermore, the Pro XL’s display is 6.8 inches in diameter, compared to the Pro’s 6.3. The Pro XL also features a 5,060mAh battery in comparison to the Pro’s slightly smaller 4,700mAh. This means the Pixel 9 Pro XL should have a better battery life.

However, you might also want to consider the differences between the “Pro” models and the standard Google Pixel 9 .

Can my Google Pixel 9 Pro XL case fit other Google Pixel models?

No, the Pixel 9 Pro XL is unique in size with regards to other Pixel phones. While there are only slight variations in size with some other models, it’s best to buy a case specifically designed for the Pixel 9 Pro XL for a precise fit.

How do I clean my Google Pixel case?

It all depends on the material it’s made from. For plastic or silicone cases, including those made from TPU, dampen a soft cloth with water, wipe the case using circular motions, and then dry with a clean, dry cloth.

For fabric cases, you can hand wash with a mild soap and water mix. Once clean, rinse with cold water, then squeeze out the excess and hang it out to dry.

A leather case requires the removal of dirt and debris with a soft, dry cloth. With another cloth dampened with lukewarm water, gently wipe away any stains. Don’t soak the leather. If the stain persists, you’ll need to use a specialized leather cleaner. Once clean, dry the leather case with the soft, dry cloth.
